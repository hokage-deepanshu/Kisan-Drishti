Frequently Asked Questions

When will the app be available?

We're actively developing it and aiming for a launch soon! Join our contact list or follow us on social media to be the first to know.

Will it support Hindi or local languages?

Absolutely! Multi-language support, including Hindi and other regional languages, is a core part of our mission to make Kisan Drishti accessible to all Indian farmers.

What if I'm not a farmer but want to get involved?

We welcome agri-enthusiasts, students, researchers, and organizations! Please reach out via our "Partner With Us" link or contact form. We're always looking for collaborators.

Will it work in low-connectivity areas?

Yes, this is a key consideration. The platform will include offline-first features for critical functionalities, designed specifically for rural connectivity challenges.

Is there a cost to use Kisan Drishti?

We are exploring various models to make it affordable and accessible. Some core features might be free, with premium options for advanced services. Details will be shared closer to launch.
